This is how I do

I work in quality assurance for 7 Days to Die, where I help improve the overall player experience by testing gameplay systems, finding bugs, and making sure new updates run smoothly. My day-to-day work includes testing mechanics like combat, crafting, zombie AI, world generation, and multiplayer features across different builds. I regularly reproduce and document issues, perform regression testing after patches, and work closely with the team to help identify problems before they reach players. Being part of the QA process has strengthened my attention to detail, communication skills, and ability to think critically about gameplay and user experience.
